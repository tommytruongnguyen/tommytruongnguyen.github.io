Tommy Truong Nguyen (he/him) is a multimedia artist currently based in San Jose, California. He enjoys designing characters and he likes to work with black, cyan, and pink. He often works with technological themes and futuristic settings. Outside of art, his hobbies are writing and video games.

space

Tommy is mostly known for his highly stylized drawings that combine his dashing line work with his passion for "cool" things. When it comes to his digital works, he's often found making websites about his current interests or trying to make an audio editing program make visual art. Nothing scares Tommy when it comes to making art; he'll tackle any artistic medium, digital or traditional, and he'll make it his own.
